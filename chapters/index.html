What are the chapters?

For DIVD.community, chapters represent branches of the community that cater to various aspects of cybersecurity. They might include local meetups, specialized interest groups, or regional chapters aiming to engage individuals within specific areas or with distinct interests in cybersecurity. These chapters offer localized events, workshops, discussions, or networking opportunities tailored to the interests and needs of their particular audience or region within the broader DIVD.community. They serve as a means to create more targeted and relevant experiences for participants while contributing to the overall goals of enhancing cybersecurity knowledge and practices.
